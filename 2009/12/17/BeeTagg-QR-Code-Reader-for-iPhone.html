BeeTagg QR Code Reader for iPhone

I've been looking for a good QR Code reader for my iPhone. I tested 8 different applications before I found one I liked. My requirements were to be free, work most of the time, and to have automatic code detection. BeeTagg Pro was the first of eight to satisfy. I suggest anyone that wants a free reader to give it a shot.
